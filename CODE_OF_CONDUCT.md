# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and considerate. Harassment, discrimination,
personal attacks, and deliberately disruptive behavior are not welcome.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions or interactions that do
not meet this standard. Serious or repeated issues may result in a temporary or
permanent ban from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through the contact options
on [Vaibhav Chopra's GitHub profile](https://github.com/sudo-vaibhav). Reports
will be reviewed fairly and kept confidential as far as practical.
